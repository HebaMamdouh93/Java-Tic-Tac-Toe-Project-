When the game starts:
1-The player chooses an option to play with computer ,network , or 2 players.
2- Incase of computer , the player enters his/her name and chooses level of difficulty and the player plays as X and computer as O
3- Incase of 2 players on same computer , each of them enters their nams and plays 
4- Incase of network, the players communicate through a server and play 
5- When the result -which is win,lose ,draw- is displayed  the player can save the game and hence the moves will be saved in the database with the names of players and Date so that the player can view his history any time.
6- On selecting a played game from history , the game steps can be shown with "auto play" option  , or step by step through "Previous" and "next" buttons .
